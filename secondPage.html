Protests Over the Murder of George Floyd

Since George Floyd was murdered, there have been many protests protesting his murder. This is a part of a New York Times article about the protests.

Article on the Murder of George Floyd
Demonstrations across the United States, which began as spontaneous eruptions of outrage after the death of George Floyd at the hands of the Minneapolis police less than two weeks ago, appeared to have cohered by Saturday into a nationwide movement protesting systemic racism. Thousands marched in big cities like New York and Seattle, and tighter groups in small towns like Vidor, Texas; Havre, Mont.; and Marion, Ohio, denouncing a broken law enforcement system marked by racial injustice.

Books I Have Read Recently

The following list describes many of the books I have been reading recently. I have been reading a lot of books recently, so the list contains only a few of them.

List of Books I Have Read Recently
Infinite Powers
Infinite Powers is a book about calculus. It talks about what calculus is and how it is very useful. It is a very good book.
The Royal Ranger
The Royal Ranger is a very good book. It is in the Ranger's Apprentice Series. Will's wife dies. To make himself feel better, he takes on an apprentice. A ranger dies mysteriously and they investigate.
The Lost Sories
The Lost Sories is the eleventh book in the Ranger's Apprentice Series. It contains many short sotories. It is a very good book. You should read it.
The Emperor of Nihon-Ja
The Emperor of Nihon-Ja is a very good book in the Ranger's Apprentice Series about a plot to take over Nihon-Ja. Horace is in Nihon-Ja. He finds out that there is a plot to take over Nihon-Ja. He helps fight the people trying to take over. His friends join him. They train some peasants to fight, and they win.
Until the End of Time
Until the End of Time is a very good book. It is about the universe. In the beginning, the author talks about the entire history of the universe, from the Big Bang to the present. Then he talks about what its future might be like.
